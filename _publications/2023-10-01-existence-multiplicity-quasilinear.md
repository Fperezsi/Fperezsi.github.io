We establish existence and multiplicity of positive solutions for a class of quasilinear elliptic problems involving the $p$-Laplace operator under nonhomogeneous Dirichlet boundary conditions. The arguments combine variational techniques (Mountain Pass-type results) with the method of sub- and super-solutions.
